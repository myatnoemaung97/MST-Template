Mandalay Campus Opening

Mandalay's Newest Jewel: M.S.T University Opens Its Doors to a Brighter Future.

M.S.T University proudly unveiled its new campus in Mandalay on September 28, 2023. The grand opening was a spectacular event, graced by new students, parents, teachers, and esteemed guests. The day's festivities included engaging activities such as a Panel Discussion, Presentations, and a Demonstration Workshop presented by our NCC global student.
Adding a global touch to the celebration, M.S.T. Scholarships were awarded to promising students of M.S.T Mandalay. Notably, social influencer Kaung Htet Zaw brought his charismatic presence as the host for the grand opening ceremony.
A special highlight of the day was the generosity extended to everyone who attended. Attendees were treated to a cashback gift of up to 15 lakhs, along with other tokens of appreciation and love. This gesture underscored M.S.T University's commitment not only to academic excellence but also to fostering a warm and welcoming community.
The opening of the new campus marked a significant chapter in M.S.T University's journey, promising a future filled with opportunities, learning, and collaboration. Congratulations to M.S.T University on this exciting milestone!
