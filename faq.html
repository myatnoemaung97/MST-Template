FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Popular Questions

Is M.S.T University exclusively focused only on Information Technology courses?

At present, M.S.T University primarily specializes in Information Technology courses. However, we have strategic plans to diversify our offerings in the future, including the introduction of business courses and other disciplines. Stay tuned for updates on our expanding curriculum.

Is it mandatory for students attending ITPEC programme to study Japanese language?

While it is not mandatory, we highly recommend that ITPEC students at M.S.T University consider studying the Japanese language. This choice can enhance job opportunities, and as part of our commitment to comprehensive education, we offer Japanese language courses as a value-added program. Ultimately, the decision to study Japanese is left to the individual student's preference.

What payment plans are available for the programs offered by M.S.T University?

At M.S.T University, we provide various payment plans to cater to different preferences. Options include upfront cash payments and convenient installment plans for the ease of our students. Additionally, we accept payments not only in cash but also through banking methods.

Are there undergraduate programs available for students who have completed NCC Level 5?

Yes, we offer several BSc programs in collaboration with esteemed institutions from the UK, Malaysia, and France. These programs serve as top-up degree programs, allowing students the flexibility to attend virtually online or in person at our university campuses.

Does M.S.T University offer postgraduate programs?

Yes, we offer Master's programs in collaboration with esteemed institutions that postgraduates can attend.
